   - Show clean, modern interface
   - Click "Post New Job"

2. **Create a job posting**
   - Title: "Senior Software Engineer"
   - Company: "Tech Corp"
   - Location: "San Francisco, CA"
   - Skills: "Python, React, AWS, Machine Learning"
   - Experience: "5+ years"
   - Description: "We're looking for a senior engineer to lead our AI initiatives..."

3. **Submit job**
   - Show success message
   - Navigate back to jobs list

### **Step 2: Resume Upload & AI Parsing (1 minute)**
1. **Navigate to Candidates page**
   - Show candidate management interface

2. **Upload resume (pre-prepared PDF)**
   - Click "Upload Resume"
   - Select sample resume (John Doe - Senior Developer)
   - Show AI parsing in real-time:
     - Name extraction
     - Skills identification
     - Experience calculation
     - Education parsing

3. **Show parsed results**
   - Highlight extracted skills
   - Show experience years
   - Display contact information

### **Step 3: AI Matching & Scoring (1 minute)**
1. **Navigate to job matches**
   - Click on the job we created
   - Show "View Candidates" or matching interface

2. **Show AI matching results**
   - Display candidate with match score (85%)
   - Show matching breakdown:
     - Skills match: 90%
     - Experience match: 80%
     - Education match: 85%
     - Location match: 100%

3. **Explain AI reasoning**
   - "Strong Python and React experience"
   - "Meets 5+ years requirement"
   - "Relevant education background"
   - "Location compatible"

### **Step 4: AI Interview (1 minute)**
1. **Schedule interview**
   - Click "Schedule Interview" for top candidate
   - Show interview setup

2. **Start AI interview**
   - Navigate to interview interface
   - Show generated questions:
     - "Tell me about your experience with microservices architecture"
     - "How would you approach scaling a Python application?"
     - "Describe a challenging technical problem you solved"

3. **Conduct interview (simulated)**
   - Show question display
   - Simulate candidate responses
   - Show timer and recording interface
   - Complete interview

4. **Show AI analysis**
   - Technical Knowledge: 8/10
   - Communication: 9/10
   - Problem Solving: 8/10
   - Overall Score: 85%
   - Recommendation: "Strong Hire"
